Unlock advanced academic literacy! Let's Go 5 serves as the golden bridge leading students toward junior advanced classes.

This course not only builds students' confidence to speak out boldly, but also guides them to read longer passages like a world encyclopedia, foster independent critical thinking, and write beautifully structured English paragraphs on their own!

📝 實戰文法基礎大公開 | Grammar Essentials

  • 精準掌握可數與不可數名詞的數量表達。
    Precisely master expression of quantity for countable and uncountable nouns.
  • 熟練運用形容詞最高級、複雜連接詞與過去時態助動詞did之實戰運用。
    Skillfully use superlative adjectives, complex conjunctions, and past tense with auxiliary verb "did".
  • 脫離單句書寫，開始訓練孩子將句子有邏輯地串聯成短段落。
    Move beyond single sentences and train students to logically connect sentences into paragraphs.
  • 具備長篇閱讀與語境思維，能從上下文推敲與理解更複雜的字彙。
    Develop advanced reading and contextual thinking to deduce complex vocabulary from contextual clues.
  • 靈活操作附屬連接詞 because 的因果邏輯及不定代名詞 one的替換技巧。
    Flexibly apply causal logic using "because" and master substitution techniques with the indefinite pronoun "one".
